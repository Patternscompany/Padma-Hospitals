Microblading Eyebrows

Microblading is a semi-permanent cosmetic tattooing technique used to enhance the appearance of eyebrows. During the microblading process, a surgeon uses a manual handheld tool to create fine, hair-like strokes in the skin, which mimic the look of natural eyebrow hairs. This technique is often used to fill in sparse areas, define the shape of the brows, or create a more symmetrical appearance.
